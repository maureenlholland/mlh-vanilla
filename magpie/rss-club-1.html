What makes me a good front-end developer?

Interview answer: I am passionate about performance and accessible user interfaces.

Honest answer: I'm impatient and easily annoyed.

Truly, I am the user that will bounce if the site takes too long to load. I am the user that will abandon a platform if it keeps changing things around and expecting me to re-learn how to use it.

As a front-end developer, I try to write the kind of code that user-me wouldn't notice, because it just works the way she wants it to work, with whatever browser and device and network connection and settings she's working with.
